Our Mission

  • Victorian Bridal is a full service bridal salon located in Waupaca, WI. For more than 25 years the shop has been dressing brides and their parties in beautiful gowns and stunning accessories. No matter what brings you to Victorian Bridal, we want you to feel as special as the event you are part of.
